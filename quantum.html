Quantum Mechanics

Quantum mechanics deals with the behavior of matter and energy at atomic and subatomic scales. It introduces concepts like wave-particle duality, uncertainty principle, and quantization.

This field revolutionized our understanding of atoms, molecules, and the fundamental forces of nature.

Quantum mechanics underpins modern technologies such as semiconductors, lasers, and quantum computing.
