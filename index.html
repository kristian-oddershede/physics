Basics of Physics

An introduction

This webpage can be used as a way to re-introduce key topics of physics as students start their bachelor's degree in physics. It can also be used to give a short recap of key equations when looking back, after having followed a course, in the exam preparation. This page focuses on the key equations and nice visuals, to create an overview for students, to supplement the material given in their courses.

Key Methodologies in Physics

Physics relies on a combination of observation, experimentation, and mathematical modeling. On this page there is a dedicated site that goes over the basics of what experiments are - with examples from an experimental bachelor project.
